Events

Annual Events

BWA hosts annual events that bring us together in community, joy, and celebration.

Meet the Freshmen

A welcoming mixer for new students to meet upperclassmen and learn about BWA's presence at MIT.

Retreat

A reflective weekend getaway focused on bonding, wellness, and sisterhood.

Black Women’s Week

A weeklong celebration of Black womanhood featuring panels, workshops, and discussions.

Banquet

The culmination of Black Women's Week, featuring performances, speeches, and awards.

Senior Sendoff

A heartfelt farewell to our graduating seniors, celebrating their legacy and impact.
